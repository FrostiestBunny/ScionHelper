Melee Attacks

  • Range: Close
  • Skill: Close Combat + Might
  • (0s + Opponent's Armor) Inflict Damage
  • (1s) Blinding: Target takes 1 Difficulty on Shoot and Throw actions
  • (4s) Critical: Deal an additional Injury
  • (2s) Disarm: Spend 1 extra success to knock the weapon away
  • (variable s) Grapple
  • (variable s) Feint: Add Enhancements to ally's attack against target equal to successes spent on this stunt
  • (1s) Trip: Knock an enemy prone
  • (variable s) Shove: Push opponent back a number of meters equal to your might per success
  • (3s) Sunder: Damage target's equipment

Ranged Attacks

  • Skill: Firearms
  • Range: Short - Dexterity; Medium - Dexterity or Cunning; Long - Cunning; Extreme - Intellect
  • Inflict Damage, Critical Hit, and Disarm same as Melee
  • (2s + x) Pin: Apply Complication 2 or more to target's next attack. They receive an Injury if it's not bought off
