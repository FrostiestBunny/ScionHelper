The Beginning of Greatness

Actions

  • Form your dice pool for most Challenges by matching a relevant Skill with any relevant Attribute
  • Spend successes to match the Difficulty of an Challenge
  • Buy off Complications with extra successes after beating the Difficulty
  • Enhancements grant extra successes only when you've rolled at least 1 success (max 5)
  • For mixed actions (doing two things at the same time), determine the dice pools and roll the smaller one for both actions
  • Leftover successes can be spent on Stunts, such as:
    • Making someone's life more complicated by adding a Complication
    • Helping an ally with their next action by adding Enhancements
    • Or simply making someone's life harder by increasing the Difficulty
  • Any extra successes not spent on Stunts will determine how awesome your action was
  • You can assist your friends by adding Enhancements to their action (max 3)
  • Complex actions encompass big projects, like investigations
    • Complex actions are made up of intervals, each of them is its own Challenge to beat
    • Completing intervals grants you Milestones
    • You succeed at the complex action by acquiring enough Milestones to match its Difficulty

Failure

  • Every time you fail you gain Momentum for the Momentum pool that's shared by everyone
  • Failing a skill you have a specialty in grants 1 extra Momentum
  • Rolling a 1 and no successes is a botch - an extreme failure that gives you 3 Momentum
  • Momentum can be spent by any of the players to:
    • Add extra dice to any roll, 1 die per Momentum, if everyone agrees
    • Add an extra interval to a complex action for 3 Momentum
    • Activate Knacks which require Momentum (no need for agreement)

Combat

  • You can take one simple action (mostly attack) and as many reflexive actions as you want in a turn
  • Moving one range band is a reflexive action (close to short, short to medium, etc)
  • Disengaging (moving away from an enemy who's at Close range to you) might sometimes require a roll and become a simple action
  • Activating a Knack is a reflexive action unless stated otherwise
  • Combat Stunts

Paths

  • You always have access to anything relevant to your path (money if you're rich, and so on)
  • Once per session, you can invoke your path to call upon a connection or a specific Contact (Skill + 2 dice)
  • Once per session, you can invoke your path to add two dice to any relevant roll

Followers

  • Your dice poll is an Attribute + Leadership or Follower's rating if higher when using your Followers
  • They always act on your turn and take up your turn
